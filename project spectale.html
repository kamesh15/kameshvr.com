IoT Based Heartbeat Monitoring and Inkling System through digital communication 

In this work Heartbeat monitoring alert via SMS and via Email , the heart rate is detected using photoplethysmograph (PPG) technique. This signal is accessed using micro-controller to determine the heart beat rate per minute (BPM). Then it sends SMS alert to the mobile phone of medical experts or patient's family members via SMS. The Heart rate data is stored in a cloud and the email automation service provided by Rackspace. When the heart rate goes abnormal, a SMS and email will be send to  phone and email ID respectively.

 Hydraulic Flood Rescuer

The model of Hydraulic Flood Rescuer is to rescue people who are drone in flood. The HFR is like a rescue  boat with hydraulic standing pillar used to hold the boat in the running water and there will be a hydraulic crane used to pick the people from flooding water.

The crane operated automatically when there is human inside the flooding water. The Rescuer has good stability to with stand in running and muddy water.
